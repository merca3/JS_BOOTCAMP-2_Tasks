The Cat Café

Enjoy Local Coffee in the Company of Some of Riga’s Friendliest Felines!

Our Cat Café provides you with a chance to relax and enjoy purr therapy in the heart of Latvia

Make your coffee break extra cuddly by spending time with some adorable feline friends! Meet cute adoptable cats from The Rescue House while enjoying award-winning locally roasted coffee in our comfortable and cozy cafe.

You’re sure to fall in love with our sweet furry friends. If you meet the cat that’s right for you and are interested in adopting, you can take them home! All our kitties are available for adoption. Please ask the staff or volunteers about the adoption process. They are happy to help you find your perfect match.

Our menu features delicious items from local bakeries and eateries, and your reservation includes one complimentary drink or food item. You can peruse our food and drink offerings here.
